About us

SCORPIO KWT was founded in 2019 by two friends, We started out our company in our own homes and worked using one printing machine and delivering all the items ourselves. Now we have our own shop and a lot of helpers and we deliver to all of Kuwait. SCORPIO KWT designs and prints on a variety of clothing items including masks. The clothing that is sold at our company is made from 100% pure cotton and of high quality. We guarantee that all clothing material is waterproof, and the colour prints are waterproof and fireproof, which means that the quality will not be ruined when using a washing machine. Our company can provide custom made prints on any type of clothing, which includes hoodies, T-shirts, caps, and masks. We also deliver to every city in Kuwait and we ensure a fast delivery service.
